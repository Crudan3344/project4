Political Aspect of Neplehsa

Although the people are organized into the Rich, Middle, and Low classes, the government gives each of them equal oppertunities in this town such as the poor citizens being able to set up markets and sell stuff to make money. This shows that the government doesn't only only favor the Rich class over the other classes. And the poor aren't kept from rising to higher classes because each citizens of every class is taxed based on their income which can be gained after the completion of education.
