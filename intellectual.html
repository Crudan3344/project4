Intellectual Aspect of Neplehsa

We have a monotheistic religion which encourages the citizens of Neplesha to follow the laws of the city. It also encourages good ethics for the people which helps and teaches them to follow the laws put in place by the council or the king. With the religio teaching the people good ethics and the do's and don'ts of the town, the citizens can live in peace with no conflict being present. Along with the religion to keep Nepleshan life peaceful, we have a strong military force to defend the city from foreign attacks and from foreign invaders.
