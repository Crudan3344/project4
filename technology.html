Technology of the Empire

(Not all tech is listed below. Only certan parts of our army. This is done out of the need for protection and security of the empire.)

With the tech we have, there is little the rebels can do to oppose us from bringing and restoring peace back into the Galaxy. (These are just a few good examples of what the empire could do. All other weapons of the empire's arsonal are classified. Those who desire to have access to this information must have achieved a certain rank. )

DeathStar

Name: deathstar

Type: Weapon (ranged)/base

Abilities: Can destroy an entire planet. (Note: Not responsible for the obliteration of Alderaan.)

Armed wtih: 300,000,000 ft radius laser cannon

Star Destroyer

Name: Star Destroyer

Type: Starship/Ranged/Weapon

Abilities: Armed with heavy cannons on each side. Can be used for blockades and defense. Great for heavy power weaponary systems.

ATAT

Name: ATAT (All.Terrain.Armored.Transport)

Type: Heavy/Armored/Land/Offensive/

Abilities: Can "stomp" out the rebellion and destroy an enemy base with only 3 blasts of the cannons.

Armed with: Cannons; 2x; mounted on left and right sides of pilot's capsule.

ATST

Name: ATST (All.Terrian.Scouting.Transport)

Type: Medium-Armored/Land Vehicle/Offensive/

Abilities: very good in speed. Pilots though may have trouble with aiming as the ATST's accuracy could be a little off sometimes due to the small windows.

Armed with: Cannons x3 (Frontal x1/Side Cannons x2)
