Branches of the Empire

Stormtrooper Corps

StormTroopers: Get into the heat of battle taking down anything that stands in the way!

-Travel across the vast galaxy scouting new planets. Get to know the galaxy well as it will soon (with your help) be the empire's own.

-Take part in heartracing ground battles. The best in the galaxy.

Imperial Naval Forces: Take part in space battles with the fleet of the empire.

-Battle in space and dock at the new Death Star

-Moves in fleets which means it's never alone

-These fleets can be used to blockade rebel forces from entering certain planets

-Included ranks: officer, pilot, commander.

Imperial Command Center (I.C.C): Consists of communications, radar detection & command center (one of these are located within the death star)

-Commands are relayed from here to the ships and other forces in the outer rim

-The communications have proven to be reliable and never fail to get information through space to our forces in space

-If you seek to support the Galactic Empire yet not to fight at the front lines, this will be the job for you.
